Build your own

Every lab shares one skeleton: an immersive console that hides Docker, a browser landing page, phased guides, and multi-arch images published to GHCR. The scaffold stamps out a new lab in that house style so you can focus on the security scenario, not the plumbing.

$ ./create-lab.sh my-new-lab "My New Lab"
